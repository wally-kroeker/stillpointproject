# "The Neural Stream" - Detailed Scene Brief

**Core Tension:** A struggling former crypto trader discovers evidence of a technological breakthrough that could reshape human coordination, forcing him to choose between professional detachment and personal investment in a story that begins affecting him physiologically in real-time.

**Emotional Arc:** From cynical professional distance → growing intellectual fascination → unconscious physiological engagement → conscious recognition of personal transformation → committed investment despite analytical resistance.

**Sensory Palette:** 
- Blue glow of six monitors casting shadows in a cramped apartment
- The subtle hum of cooling fans and electrical equipment 
- Fingertips on mechanical keyboard, muscle memory from trading days
- Neural interface's gentle pressure at the temple, warmth when active

**Key Beats - Detailed Stream Timeline:**

**Stream Opening Ritual**

- Six monitors displaying: main stream interface, chat, research databases, neural feedback dashboard, subscriber metrics, backup recording

- Chat already active: mix of regulars (CryptoRefugee2029, DisplacedCoder, StreamingGrandma) and newcomers

- Marcus explains research methodology: economic data analysis, government reports, social media monitoring
- **Tension introduced:** Chat immediately splits on community discussion—some defensive about commune life, others dismissive
