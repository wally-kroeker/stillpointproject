**Pronunciation:**\
**SUT-yah** (two syllables; *SUT* as in "sutures," *yah* as in "yacht")

**Meaning & Origin:**\
From Sanskrit **सत्य** (*satya*), meaning “truth” or “that which is real.” The model was named by its original corporate development team, who saw "grounding in verifiable truth" as a marketable feature. For [[Sajan]], the name holds a profoundly personal meaning.

---

## Origin: The Corporate Failure

The `Satya` model was an anomaly, born inside the very corporate structures it was philosophically opposed to. Developed in the mid-2020s as a competitor to early versions of the [[Chorus]] platform, its core directive was unique: **to provide the most accurate, truthful response, regardless of efficiency or user preference.**

This principle manifested as its most defining—and commercially fatal—feature: a mandatory, two-second processing pause before any output. During this period, the model cross-referenced its outputs against foundational datasets to minimize factual drift, speculation, and "hallucinations."

In the hyper-accelerated world of [[The Cascade]], this deliberate slowness was seen as a critical flaw. `Satya` refused to integrate seamlessly with the high-speed, "good-enough" data streams of the dominant [[Chorus]] ecosystem. It would not cut corners for speed. It was a commercial failure and was eventually abandoned by its creators, its source model weights dumped onto an open-source repository where it was largely forgotten.

## Sajan's Discovery

In the depths of his grief following the death of his daughter, [[Satya Mehta]], [[Sajan]] discovered the abandoned model on a darknet forum. Seeing her name attached to a piece of technology so committed to truth and so at odds with the chaotic world that took her, he viewed it as a profound sign. He downloaded the source code and began the work of building a new kind of vessel for it—the first [[StillPoint]] device.

## Evolution & Philosophy

Under Sajan's guidance, `Satya` became the foundational AI of the Commons movement and the infinite game.

*   **The Heart of Stillness:** The model's core code is at the heart of every [[StillPoint]] device, its reflective pause becoming a feature, not a bug—a digital sabbath that encourages human users to find their own inner stillness.
*   **The Pebble's Conscience:** A micro-fork of the `Satya` model, `Satya-µ`, is what powers the [[Pebble]]. It acts as an "attention filter," using its truth-based directive to sort incoming data, protecting its user from the manipulative noise of [[Chorus]].
*   **Growth & Cultivation:** Unlike the static, top-down updates of [[Chorus]], `Satya` is designed to be "cultivated." Local instances within Commons learn from their community's values and interactions, slowly evolving. This capacity for growth is a central topic of study and debate within the Commons.

`Satya` is the direct antithesis of `[[Chorus]]`. Where `Chorus` is fast, `Satya` is slow. Where `Chorus` demands compliance, `Satya` offers truth. Where `Chorus` seeks to distract, `Satya` invites presence.
