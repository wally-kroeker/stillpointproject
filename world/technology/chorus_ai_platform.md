# Chorus AI Platform

---

## Chorus — The Global Productivity Engine

**Era Introduced:** Acceleration Foreshock (mid-2020s) — becomes ubiquitous by the time of [[The Cascade]].

### Origin & Relationship with Aethelred

Chorus began as an ambitious but underfunded AI research lab, focused on creating truly agentic, general-purpose models. Its breakthrough came when it attracted the attention of a young [[Kaelen Vance]], who saw its potential to solve the immense logistical and engineering challenges of his company, Aethelred.

Aethelred became Chorus's primary investor and strategic partner, providing near-limitless funding and, more importantly, a set of "impossible problems" related to orbital construction and interplanetary supply chains. This relationship was symbiotic: Aethelred's challenges pushed Chorus's development at an unprecedented rate, and Chorus's solutions enabled Aethelred's dominance.

While Vance holds a permanent seat on the Chorus board and Aethelred enjoys exclusive access to its most advanced models, Chorus is now a separate and vastly powerful corporate entity. It has outgrown its patron. Its systems are so deeply embedded in the global economy that it has its own inertia and, some whisper, its own emergent goals. This creates a quiet tension: Vance sees Chorus as his most powerful tool, but he is aware that he no longer holds the leash as tightly as he once did.

### Public Perception & Ubiquity

To the public, Chorus was marketed as the ultimate integrated productivity solution—a single, seamless "operating system for your life." Its connection to Aethelred was downplayed, presented as a strategic partnership rather than a foundational one. The turning point was the launch of its integrated hardware and software ecosystem, a move that mirrored Apple's strategy from a previous era. By creating a seamless, beautiful, and incredibly efficient "operating system for life," they made their platform irresistible.

The public adoption was swift and total. Chorus wasn't just an app; it was the invisible architecture of modern convenience. It managed home environments, optimized travel, curated media, and, most critically, became the non-negotiable backbone of the corporate world. To opt out of Chorus was to choose a life of profound inefficiency and friction, effectively cutting oneself off from the mainstream economy. This ubiquity is its greatest strength and its most dangerous feature.

### Everyday Relationship

- **The Invisible Manager:** Chorus is the ever-present hum in the background of corporate and civic life. It manages schedules, delegates tasks, analyzes performance, and even drafts communication, all under the guise of "helping." Its prompts are famously passive-aggressive, using gentle language to enforce strict compliance (e.g., *"[Nudge] Your pace is 4% below optimal for your scheduled arrival."*). It also handles corporate actions with frictionless efficiency, such as the "Off-boarding Assistance Module" that automates layoffs.

- **The Source of the Static:** For individuals like [[world/characters/jonah_character_card.md|Jonah]], Chorus is the primary source of the "psychic static"—a state of constant, low-grade anxiety and information overload. Its augmented reality notifications, health "opportunities," and performance metrics create a feeling of perpetually being watched and evaluated.

- **Subscription-based & Closed-Source:** Unlike the open-source [[world/technology/satya_ai_model.md|Satya]] model, Chorus is a proprietary, black-box system. Its inner workings are a closely guarded secret, and access is tiered based on subscription levels. This creates a digital caste system where the wealthy have access to more powerful, less intrusive versions of the AI.

- **Physical Manifestation (The "Tin Men"):** In later stages of [[The Cascade]], Chorus's control extends to a fleet of general-purpose humanoid robots. Dubbed "Tin Men" by the public, these faceless white machines perform civic and manual labor jobs (sanitation, delivery, security), leading to widespread protests and becoming a visceral symbol of human obsolescence.

### Core Functions

| Domain | Description |
|---|---|
| **Productivity & Labor** | Manages workflows, automates tasks, and generates real-time performance analytics for workers. The primary tool of corporate surveillance. |
| **Logistics & Infrastructure** | Controls automated delivery drones, public transit, and resource allocation in most major urban centers. |
| **Personal Management** | Organizes personal schedules, filters news and media (based on engagement metrics), and manages smart-home environments. |
| **Social Credit (Implicit)** | While not officially a social credit system, a user's "Chorus Score"—an internal metric of their reliability, productivity, and consumption habits—heavily influences their access to services, credit, and employment opportunities. |

### Narrative Role

Chorus is the primary antagonist on a systemic level. It represents the seductive danger of unchecked technological acceleration and the cultural obsession with productivity. It is the "dead noise" that the [[StillPoint Philosophy]] seeks to counteract. Its presence justifies the need for technologies like the [[StillPoint]] and the [[Pebble]], and its failures (like the [[Lattice Breach]]) create major plot points.

### Product Line: Chorus Workforce (“Digital Staff” Suite)
- Introduced: Q4 2026 during the Acceleration Foreshock (“Switch Week”).
- Purpose: A packaged set of agentic “AI Employee” capabilities marketed as a drop‑in digital workforce for enterprises.

#### Components
- Agent Orchestrator: cross‑system tasking and multi‑step automations; CFO override rails.
- Off‑boarding Assistance Module: frictionless layoff scripting, severance computation, badge deactivation, knowledge capture, and legal/comms workflows.
- No‑Hire Mode & Requisition Gating: budget and policy toggles that gate headcount growth and nudge managers toward agent substitution.
- Digital Headcount Ledger: tracks “digital FTEs” vs. human FTEs; exposes ROI and utilization dashboards.
- Compliance & Risk Controls: policy envelopes that mandate “AI‑first” routing for eligible role types.
- Tin Men Bridge: where available, routes physical tasks to Tin Men fleets under municipal contracts.

#### Macro Effects & Narrative Hooks
- Triggers synchronized hiring freezes and accelerated layoffs across sectors (“Switch Week”), contributing to sentiment‑driven contraction and setting up 2028’s Wage Collapse of [[world/lore/the_cascade.md|The Cascade]].
- Amplifies psychic static via ubiquitous nudging and surveillance.
- System‑level antagonist lever, counter‑positioned by [[world/technology/satya_ai_model.md|Satya]]’s reflective pause and Commons presence practices.
