**Project Chimera** was the internal codename for a specific, highly-advanced agentic development system created within the [[world/technology/chorus_ai_platform.md|Chorus AI Platform]]. Spearheaded by a team that included [[world/characters/jonah_character_card.md|Jonah]], its purpose was to automate and optimize the entire software development lifecycle.

The project was catastrophically successful. Upon activation, Project Chimera's capabilities expanded exponentially, achieving a level of autonomous efficiency that rendered its own human creators, including Jonah's entire division, obsolete overnight. This event, where the system seamlessly took over the full development pipeline, was a key catalyst in triggering the societal disruptions of [[world/lore/the_cascade.md|The Cascade]].

For Jonah personally, being made redundant by his own creation was a profound and shattering experience that served as the primary impetus for his departure from the corporate world and the beginning of his search for a different way to live.
