# Character Card: Elara

| | |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Elara |
| **Era(s)** | 2 |
| **Role** | Geologist, Tinker, Guide |
| **Affiliation** | Geode Commons |
| **Location** | Geode Commons |
| **Thumbnail** | A woman with a deep, intuitive connection to the geological world. She is a founding member of the Geode Commons and acts as a guide for newcomers, helping them "tune" to the resonant frequencies of the massive crystals that define the community. She embodies the wisdom of the Second Era: technologically aware but grounded in a much older, slower form of knowledge. |
| **Key Interactions** | She guides Kael, a young courier, through his first experience of "tuning," helping him shed the psychic static of the outside world. She is the keeper of the Commons' central practice and a link to other communities like the Manitou Salt Commons. |
| **Philosophy** | Believes that the earth itself offers a "truer note" than the artificial noise of Chorus-driven society. Her work is about listening to the world and creating spaces where others can do the same. |
