| | |
|---|---|

| **Location** | Mobile; encountered at [[Geode Commons]] |
| **Thumbnail** | A young, capable courier accustomed to the "finite game" of schedules and deliveries. He carries the residual psychic static of the cities and is initially resistant to the slower, more contemplative pace of the Commons. His journey represents the struggle of a generation caught between the old world's frantic efficiency and the new world's emphasis on presence. |
| **Key Interactions** | His encounter with [[Elara]] at the [[Geode Commons]] serves as his introduction to the "infinite game" and the practice of "tuning" to a deeper resonance than the digital noise he's used to. |
| **Artifacts** | Standard-issue [[Courier Guild]] e-bike and lock-case. Carries a Guild-slate for manifests. Is gifted a simple river stone by [[Elara]] as a reminder of the "true signal." |
