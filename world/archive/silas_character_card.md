# Character Card: Silas

| | |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Silas |
| **Era(s)** | 2 |
| **Role** | Stone-Singer, Purist Philosopher |
| **Affiliation** | [[Geode Commons]] (leader of the "Stone-Singer" faction) |
| **Location** | [[Geode Commons]] |
| **Thumbnail** | A gaunt, patient man with hands calloused from direct contact with stone. Silas is the primary advocate for the "slow path" of attunement, believing that true harmony can only be found through unassisted, patient listening. He is not opposed to technology, but is deeply wary of its potential to become a spiritual crutch, creating an illusion of presence rather than fostering the real thing. |
| **Key Interactions** | He provides the philosophical counterpoint to [[Elara]]'s pragmatism. Their ongoing, respectful debate forms the core of the Commons' social and spiritual dynamic. He often takes apprentices who wish to learn the more demanding, tech-free methods of tuning. |
| **Philosophy** | "The amplifier lets you hear the song, but patience lets you become the singer." He believes the struggle and the waiting are essential parts of the practice, not obstacles to be overcome with technology. |
