### **Background and The Riverbend Dream**

Chloë is a fiercely passionate artist and the emotional anchor of her friend group with [[world/characters/jonah_character_card.md|Jonah]] and [[world/characters/liam_character_card.md|Liam]]. Long before the events of [[The Cascade]], she was the primary driver behind [[world/lore/the_riverbend_dream_origins.md|The Riverbend Dream]], their shared vision for a life outside the encroaching world of hyper-optimization.

As the group's self-appointed historian, she researched the successes and failures of past intentional communities, providing the intellectual and ethical backbone for their dream. Her focus was always on the social and cultural infrastructure—the shared meals, rituals, and creative expressions that would serve as the community's glue. She envisioned a life where art was not a commodity but a lived, communal experience, with a "terroir" drawn from the land itself.

---

### **Catalyst for Change: The Desecration of Genius**

For Chloë, the crisis of [[The Cascade]] is not primarily economic but spiritual. She is horrified by the rise of AI-generated art, viewing it as a form of theft that "steals the struggle." The tech industry's claim to have "democratized genius" is, to her, a desecration. She believes the doubt, frustration, and happy accidents of the creative process are the entire point, the very soil from which human art grows.

Her response to the rise of the "Tin Men" and the broader societal shift is not one of despair, but of fierce, protective anger. She becomes a "war artist," her sketchbook filled with jagged, emotional portraits of the people being displaced, documenting the human casualties of the silent conflict with the machine.

---

### **Role in the Story**

Chloë represents the fight for the soul of human experience in a world obsessed with perfect, dead copies. She is the cultural and artistic heart of the nascent Commons, constantly reminding the group of *why* they are building their alternative, not just *what* they are building.

While [[world/characters/liam_character_card.md|Liam]] builds the physical structures and [[world/characters/jonah_character_card.md|Jonah]] navigates the technological challenges, Chloë builds the culture of the [[world/locations/riverbend_commons_location_card.md|Riverbend Commons]]. She is the one who insists on the first fire, who encourages the sharing of stories, and who will eventually establish the pottery studio and other creative spaces that make the Commons a place of messy, vibrant, human life, not just a refuge. She transforms the "lifeboat" of [[world/lore/the_riverbend_dream_origins.md|The Riverbend Dream]] into a "harbor" by focusing on the human connections that make it a true community.

**Symbol:**
Hands smudged with clay or charcoal. It represents her direct, tangible connection to her work and her rejection of the sterile, untouchable world of digital creation.
