# Lore Card: The Cascade

**Era:** 2028 – 2036 CE

**Overview:** The Cascade was a period of profound societal fracture defined by cascading failures across economic, political, and social systems. It was triggered by the uncontrollable, exponential growth of artificial intelligence and automation, which set off a chain reaction of events that destabilized the old world order and set the stage for the emergence of the [[world/lore/stillpoint_philosophy.md|StillPoint Philosophy]]. Though the sharpest breaks occur in 2028–2036, the behavioral primers were laid during the Acceleration Foreshock (2025–2027).

---

### Core Characteristics

*   **Cascading Acceleration:** The era was defined by runaway technological growth, particularly AI services whose marginal cost approached zero while displacing human labor entirely. This created a cascade of consequences: soaring productivity, mass labor displacement, and a rapidly widening wealth gap that shattered economic stability.

*   **Sentiment Cascades:** Memetic synchronization of anxiety, price‑shock memes, and executive guidance that compresses timelines—freezing hiring, accelerating layoffs, and stalling consumption faster than fundamentals move. Prefigured by 2026–27 “Switch Week” and the “$20 Diet Coke” meme; amplified by [[world/technology/chorus_ai_platform.md|Chorus]] nudges; later countered by [[world/technology/satya_ai_model.md|Satya]]’s reflective pause and presence practices within Commons.

*   **Cascading Collapse of Meaning:** As old certainties were swept away, traditional institutions and belief systems failed, leading to a "profound collapse of traditional sources of meaning." This vacuum was filled with fragmented belief systems, intense tribalism, and a pervasive sense of existential dread.

*   **The Finite Game Mindset:** Society was dominated by a [[Infinite and Finite Games|finite game]] worldview—a hyper-individualistic, competitive struggle for power and resources. This "separate-self narrative" accelerated the collapse, as it prevented the collective action needed to manage the cascading crises.

*   **Political Instability:** The period saw extreme political polarization and civil unrest, with democratic institutions pushed to their breaking point.

### Key Events

*   **2028:** The first major cascade hits as AI-driven market shifts crash global wages, eroding public trust.
*   **2029:** Political turmoil intensifies. [[Sajan]]'s daughter is killed in the crossfire of a protest, a tragedy that directly leads to the invention of the first [[StillPoint]] prototype.
*   **2032:** The first "Resting Place" opens in a reclaimed Minneapolis megachurch, becoming the seed of [[Hawthorn Commons]] and offering a refuge from the chaos.
*   **2036:** The "StillPoint Gathering" demonstrates the power of mass bio-coherence, marking a turning point and the beginning of a counter-cascade toward integration and presence.

The Cascade serves as the crucible in which the old world, built on distraction and division, was washed away, creating the conditions for a new society to arise from the wreckage.
