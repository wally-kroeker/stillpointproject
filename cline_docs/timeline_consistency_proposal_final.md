- **Riverbend Commons** = Well-functioning intentional community founded by Jonah/Liam/Chloë + Maren/Ben
- **Satya AI** doesn't exist until ~2027, rediscovered by Sajan post-2029
- **AI tension at Riverbend** happens AFTER Jonah returns from The Oaks (2036)
- **Maren's departure** must set up her mentoring Loria at Mountain Sanctuary

## Timeline Issues to Resolve

1. **Missing 2028:** World Bible shows 2028 wage collapse when AI labor became cheaper than infrastructure but outline jumps 2027→2029
2. **Era naming mismatch:** "Era 1 — The Cascade" vs. "Part 2: The Birth of the StillPoint"  
3. **Need Maren departure scene** to bridge her Riverbend → Mountain Sanctuary transition

## Proposed Solution: Clean Bridging Content

### **Revised Timeline Structure**

**Part 1: Acceleration Foreshock (2025 – 2028)**
- Current chapters 1-7 + new 2028 content
- E1C07 "The First Fire" = Riverbend Commons founding (Late 2027)
- Shows Riverbend as stable, successful community through 2028 crisis

**Part 2: Era 1 — The Cascade (2029 - 2036)**
- Adopts World Bible naming
- Boston protests → StillPoint invention → Jonah's Oaks journey
- ENDS with Jonah returning to Riverbend with new ideas (sets up future AI tensions)

### **New Bridging Chapters (2028)**

#### **Chapter 8: The Refuge (Early 2028)**
**Concept:** The World Bible's wage collapse hits, but Riverbend thrives as a refuge.
**Key Elements:**
- AI cognitive labor becomes functionally free, crashing wages across Canada
- Displaced workers arrive at Riverbend seeking shelter
- Community demonstrates its resilience and hospitality
- Shows Riverbend as a MODEL intentional community, not struggling
- Maren's steady presence helps integrate newcomers

#### **Chapter 9: The Teaching (Mid 2028)**
**Concept:** Riverbend becomes a learning center for Commons principles.
**Key Elements:**
- Other communities send representatives to learn from Riverbend
- Maren naturally becomes the teacher, sharing presence practices
- Community develops protocols for resource sharing, decision-making
- Word spreads about "the Riverbend way" of doing things
- Establishes Riverbend's reputation in growing underground network

#### **Chapter 10: The Calling (Late 2028)**
**Concept:** Maren receives a mysterious calling to leave.
**Key Elements:**
- Maren begins having dreams/visions about mountains and a specific place
- She speaks of "someone who will need what I can offer"
- Community resists her leaving - she's become central to their identity
- Maren insists she must go, that her work at Riverbend is complete
- **Sets up Loria eventually finding her at Mountain Sanctuary**

#### **Chapter 11: The Departure (Early 2029)**
**Concept:** Maren's mysterious departure and community adaptation.
**Key Elements:**
- Touching farewell scene - Maren passes her thermos to someone else
- Community realizes they must embody her teachings, not depend on her presence
- Jonah, Liam, Chloë step into leadership roles more fully
- Bridge to political crisis and Boston protests
- **Maren heads to Mountain Sanctuary where she'll later mentor Loria**

### **Riverbend Evolution (Corrected)**

**Phase 1 (Late 2027):** Founding
- E1C07 "The First Fire" = founding ceremony
- Small group: Jonah/Liam/Chloë + Maren/Ben + others from park

**Phase 2 (2028):** Growth and reputation
- Becomes refuge during wage collapse
- Develops into model intentional community
- Maren's teaching role emerges naturally
- Gains reputation in underground Commons network

**Phase 3 (2029-2035):** Stability and networking
- Community continues thriving after Maren's departure
- Core leadership by Jonah/Liam/Chloë
- Connected to broader movement through networks
- Sends Jonah to The Oaks as their representative

**Phase 4 (Post-2036):** Technology tensions
- Jonah returns from Oaks with StillPoint knowledge
- Community debates adopting Satya AI and other technologies
- Tests their principles: can technology serve their values?
- **This becomes Era 2 storyline, not Era 1**
