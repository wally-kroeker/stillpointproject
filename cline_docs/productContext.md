# Product Context: The StillPoint Saga

## 1. Core Vision

This project aims to produce a single, cohesive novel that spans the three distinct eras outlined in the `still_point_world_bible.md`. The central narrative will explore a hopeful vision for humanity's future, showing a path of integration and contemplative renaissance in the face of accelerating technological change, particularly with Artificial Intelligence.

## 2. Problem Solved

The novel serves as a narrative counterpoint to dystopian AI futures. It seeks to answer the question: "How can humanity slow down, find presence, and build a more whole society when technology is pushing for ever-increasing speed and distraction?" It will demonstrate that grassroots, human-centric technology and a focus on inner presence are viable paths forward.

## 3. How It Should Work

The story will follow a set of core characters across the three eras:

*   **Era 1 (Near-Future Crisis):** Establishes the core conflict, introduces the catalytic invention of the StillPoint device, and shows the societal fractures caused by unchecked technological acceleration and capitalism.
*   **Era 2 (Transitional):** Explores the societal shift as the principles of StillPoint gain traction, new forms of community emerge (like the Commons), and humanity begins to integrate the new technological landscape in a healthier way.
*   **Era 3 (Luminous Presence):** Depicts the mature, integrated society where technology has receded into the background, and a culture of presence and contemplation is the norm.

The narrative will weave together the personal journeys of the characters with the broader societal and technological evolution, staying true to the themes and style defined in the world bible.
