# The Neural Stream

These patterns were then translated through specialized neuromorphic chips into data streams that his audience could experience as emotional resonance rather than mere observation.

The technology was experimental, glitchy, and expensive—which was exactly why it commanded premium prices in the 2037 attention economy. Marcus's subscribers paid not just for his insights, but for the privilege of feeling his discoveries alongside him, experiencing the texture of human curiosity as it unfolded through the neural interface.

The soft hum of cooling fans provided a constant backdrop, punctuated by the mechanical click of his keyboard—a vintage model from his cryptocurrency trading days when milliseconds meant fortunes and the tactile feedback of Cherry MX switches gave him confidence in his rapid-fire buy and sell decisions. Those days felt like a lifetime ago, though only eight years had passed since the 2028 wage collapse had wiped out his portfolio and forced him to rebuild his life from the ground up.

Open-source specifications prevent corporate enclosure, with radical transparency protocols built into the technology's fundamental architecture.
